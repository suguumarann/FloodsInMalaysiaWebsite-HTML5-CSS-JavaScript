— FLOODS IN MALAYSIA —

 
The majority of Malaysians are affected by flood every time it happens. As monsoon season approaches, flash flood always becomes a common sight in Malaysia and it is a common waking nightmare for many people here. Flash floods inundate the roads and houses, leaving the people high-and-dry as they find themselves stuck in the middle of an unexpected swamp or forced to leave their homes as they become a high-tide zone in the middle of formerly-dry land. And to make matters worse, it would seem that flash floods are becoming more and more frequent in Malaysia. So what is causing these floods to rise in frequency? And what can be done in order to mitigate them? On this website, we will take a look at the causes of floods, provide donations to those who are affected by these deadly floods at the same time provide various assistance to those who are really in need.
